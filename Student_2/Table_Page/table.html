Water & Sanitation Awareness

Awareness Topic Description Key Facts Prevention/Action Steps
Safe Drinking Water Importance of consuming clean water 1 in 3 people lack access to safe drinking water Boil/filter water, avoid stagnant sources
Water Conservation Reducing unnecessary water waste A leaking tap can waste over 3,000 liters/year Fix leaks, use water-saving appliances
Proper Sanitation Hygienic practices for public health Poor sanitation causes 432,000 deaths annually Build proper toilets, manage waste effectively
Wastewater Treatment Treating used water before disposal 80% of wastewater is released untreated Use treatment plants, avoid dumping chemicals
Handwashing & Hygiene Preventing disease through proper hygiene Handwashing reduces diarrheal diseases by 40% Use soap, wash hands regularly
Rainwater Harvesting Collecting and storing rainwater Can save up to 50% of household water needs Install rainwater collection systems
Impact of Water Pollution How pollution affects water bodies 2 billion people use contaminated water Avoid dumping waste in water sources
Sustainable Water Usage Using water efficiently Agriculture consumes 70% of freshwater Drip irrigation, water-efficient fixtures
Toilet & Waste Management Proper waste disposal for hygiene 494 million people still practice open defecation Promote toilet construction & awareness
Plastic Pollution & Water How plastic harms water sources 1 million plastic bottles are bought every minute Reduce plastic use, recycle responsibly
